Research Opportunities



I am actively seeking graduate students (MSc or PhD) to join my lab in the Department of Geography at Western University in London, Ontario, Canada beginning September 2020. Potential Topics Include:
  • Avian migration and big data analytics
  • Human-wildlife disturbance
  • Remote sensing in movement ecology
  • Human mobility

I expect interested students students to have strong quantitative skills (e.g., statistics, GIS, spatial analysis) along with an ability to code (i.e., R or Python).

Potential Students can express interest by emailing me an up-to-date CV and example piece of work to me (jed.long AT uwo.ca).
