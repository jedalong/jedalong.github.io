Biography

I have a BSc in Physical Science from the University of Guelph, Canada, an Advanced Diploma in Geographic Information Systems (GIS) from the Centre of Geographic Sciences, Canada, and an MSc and PhD in Geography from the University of Victoria, Canada where I was part of the Spatial Pattern Analysis & Research lab(SPAR). I have been a Lecturer in the School of Geography & Geosciences at the University of St Andrews since September 2013. As a result, I am hopeful my golf handicap will soon improve.
