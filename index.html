Research

Broadly, I am interested in the development of novel forms of spatial and space-time analysis - specifically much of my research involves using geographic information systems (GIS) and other spatial analysis techniques to solve ecological and environmental problems. Currently, I focus largely on the development of quantitative methods for exploring spatial patterns movement data, e.g., data obtained through GPS tracking. I also remain active in other research areas relating more broadly to GIScience; including spatial modelling, volunteered geographic information (and other non-traditional datasets), and map comparison. Finally, as a huge sports fan, I am fascinated by ways spatial data and analysis can be used in sports analytics.
